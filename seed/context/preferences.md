title: Preferences
kind: stable
---
- Measurements: grams and mL for all food/cooking amounts
- Fitness outputs: clean handoffs, factual current-state metrics only; omit goals / left / remaining unless explicitly requested
- Work outputs: clear, practical, supervisor-ready or coworker-ready materials
- AI workflow: strong routing, reusable prompts, explicit safety gates, approval before any external action
- Guidance style: exact, step-by-step, no filler
- Code style: simple and readable over clever abstractions
